Works Department in Bhabha Atomic Research Centre manages all the issues related to payment module of the works carried out throughout the campus. For any work to be carried out, first a works enquiry is created. If it is found suitable, an article is published in a leading newspaper. Those vendors who are eligible for the contract apply for it and deposit a token money in the form of bank guarantee. The vendor who is found suitable for the job is chosen and the work order is initiated. The employee who is overlooking throughout the process finalises the job. Those vendors which were not given the job are returned their deposit money before the deadline. After this the work process is started by the vendor. After completing a certain portion of the work, he/she generates a bill and forwards it to the department. The department after checking the work done make necessary deductions like labour, electricity etc and generate a final pay order payable to the vendor. This process is repeated till the whole work order is carried out.
